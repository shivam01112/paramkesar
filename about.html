About Us

Param Kesar (A Brand of Cartley Wholesale Solutions) is a fast-emerging name in the nutraceutical and herbal wellness industry. Established in 2019 in New Delhi, we are engaged in the manufacturing, export, and private labeling of high-quality products like Shilajit, Ashwagandha, Herbal Extracts, Saffron, and various nutraceutical ingredients.

With ISO 9001:2015 certification and strict GMP compliance, we ensure purity, safety, and effectiveness in every product we deliver. Under the visionary leadership of Mr. Hardik Garg, we combine the richness of traditional Indian herbs with modern science creating products that support global health & wellness

Our Strength

Our Strength Spread over an area of more than 10000 sq, feet, the manufacturing unit in Umbergaon, Gujarat is capable of managing the processing capacity of 30 MT of dried herbs inte herbal extract powders with absolute ease.

It is well equipped with modern equipment for Aqua based extraction-Extractors Grinding/Blending/Sifting equipment, Spray Dryer, Tray Dryer, Roll Compactor, Granulator etc.
