About Us

Param Kesar (A Brand of Cartley Wholesale Solutions) is a fast-emerging name in the nutraceutical and herbal wellness industry. Established in 2019 in New Delhi, we are engaged in the manufacturing, export, and private labeling of high-quality products like Shilajit, Ashwagandha, Herbal Extracts, Saffron, and various nutraceutical ingredients.

With ISO 9001:2015 certification and strict GMP compliance, we ensure purity, safety, and effectiveness in every product we deliver. Under the visionary leadership of Mr. Hardik Garg, we combine the richness of traditional Indian herbs with modern science creating products that support global health & wellness.

Our Process


From Nature to Innovation: A Trusted nutraceuticals Journey

At Param Kesar, our nutraceuticals products are crafted through a precise and thoughtful process where nature’s purity meets modern science. From sourcing potent herbs and botanical extracts to advanced purification and GMP-certified packaging — every product, whether it’s a botanical extract, vitamin blend, or mineral supplement, is developed to meet the highest quality standards. We provide end-to-end solutions, from raw ingredients to ready-to-label capsules, tablets, powders, and more empowering your brand with trusted & wellness.

Step 1:

Trusted Sourcing

We responsibly source high-quality nutraceutical ingredients — including herbs, vitamins, and functional actives — from certified farms and trusted suppliers, ensuring purity from the origin.

Step 2:

Advanced Processing & Extraction

Using modern techniques like drying, grinding, and solvent-free extraction, we convert raw materials into potent powders, extracts, resins, and other forms — while preserving their natural properties.

Step 3:

Rigorous Testing & Quality Control

Each batch undergoes strict lab testing to ensure safety, purity, and compliance with global standards — checking for heavy metals, microbes, and active ingredients.

Step 4:

Custom Formulation & Product Development

Our R&D team creates specialized blends for health goals such as energy, immunity, digestion, and stress relief — tailored to client requirements.

Step 5:

Packaging & Private Labeling

We offer flexible packaging solutions like jars, sachets, bottles, and capsules with full branding and export-ready labeling support.

Step 6:

Shipping & Long-Term Support

We ensure safe, on-time delivery worldwide and build lasting partnerships by supporting innovation, reordering, and business growth.
