About Us

Param Kesar is a social business venture dedicated to building a sustainable and scalable model focused on holistic rural development. Our mission is to economically and socially uplift small and marginal farmers at the grassroots level. We encourage farmers in remote villages to cultivate high-value herbs, crops, and medicinal plants. From farming to processing, branding, and market access — we offer complete support through an end-to-end supply chain, ensuring both farmer welfare and consumer wellness.

As a quality-driven organization, we provide premium herbal products developed under the guidance of strict quality experts. Every product is carefully tested using advanced techniques and meets international standards.

From Himalayas to Your Brand: A Trusted Shilajit Journey

At Param Kesar, Shilajit begins its path in the majestic Himalayan ranges and reaches your brand through a process rooted in tradition and refined by science. Our careful sourcing, advanced purification, and quality-controlled packaging ensure that every batch retains its original strength and purity. We offer a complete solution—from raw material to ready-to-label product—crafted to elevate your brand with the power of nature.

Step 1:

Ethical Sourcing

Our journey starts in the elevated regions Of the Himalayas, where we work closely with local gatherers to source Shilajit ethically and responsibly. Through time-honored and sustainable methods, we maintain its natural purity and contribute to the well-being Of the mountain communities connected to this ancient process.
