Shilajit is a natural mineral-rich resin found in Himalayan rocks, used for centuries in Ayurvedic medicine. It is widely used to treat fatigue, infertility, weakness, inflammation, aging, and memory loss. Shilajit enhances energy, immunity, and physical performance. It is applied in both oral and topical forms and is also used in health supplements and rejuvenation products.

Shilajit contains fulvic acid, humic acid, minerals, and antioxidants. It boosts the absorption of nutrients, improves stamina, and balances hormones. Shilajit is also used in men's health supplements, immunity boosters, and anti-aging formulas.

Benefits:
  • Shilajit is a natural energy booster that increases ATP production and reduces fatigue.
  • It contains fulvic acid which enhances brain function and slows aging.
  • It supports male fertility by improving testosterone levels and sperm count.
  • It improves nutrient absorption and helps regulate blood sugar.
  • It has strong antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties that protect organs and joints.
  • Rich in trace minerals like iron, zinc, magnesium, and selenium.
